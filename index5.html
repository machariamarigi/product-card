Tuna Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 can (185g) tuna in brine or oil, drained
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped celery
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, combine tuna, mayonnaise, and celery.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Mix well until all ingredients are combined.
  4. Serve on bread, crackers, or lettuce leaves.
  5. Optional: add a squeeze of lemon juice or a sprinkle of chopped fresh herbs for extra flavor.
